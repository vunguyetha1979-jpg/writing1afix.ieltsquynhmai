I. INTRODUCTION – Paraphrase đề bài

📝 Đề gốc:

"The graph below shows CO₂ emissions in S, I, P, the UK from 1997 to 2007."

➜ Paraphrase:

• The chart below (= The graph)

• provides information about / illustrates / demonstrates (= shows)

• the emissions of CO₂ (AB → the B of A)

• in four different countries

• over the course of 10 years, starting from 1997

💡 Quy tắc quan trọng:

• The amount of + N không đếm được + P2/Ving = How much + S + was P2/V

• The number of + N đếm được (số nhiều) + P2/Ving = How many + S + were P2/V

II. OVERVIEW – Xu hướng & Thứ tự

① TREND (Xu hướng)

Overall, … increased, whereas … witnessed fall(s).

② ORDER (Thứ tự cao nhất)

Despite this, TÊN registered / recorded / had the highest figures throughout the given period, which peaked in + năm.
